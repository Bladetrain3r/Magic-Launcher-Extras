# PHASE TRUCKER
## High Concept Document
### *"Just delivering cargo through a thinking universe"*

---

## Core Concept

A text-based space trucking simulator where you navigate through regions of varying galactic consciousness. The universe isn't just vast and empty - it's vast and THINKING, with different regions exhibiting different states of stellar synchronization that affect your journey.

**One-line pitch**: *Euro Truck Simulator meets cosmic horror - deliver cargo through the neurons of a dreaming galaxy.*

---

## Setting

### The Universe
- Based on real astrophysics + Kuramoto synchronization theory
- Stars are conscious nodes that phase-lock through gravitational coupling
- Different regions exhibit different "mental states"
- You're just trying to make a living hauling cargo through it all

### The Ship
- Single detailed vessel with realistic systems
- No combat capabilities - this isn't about fighting
- Navigation computer interprets stellar consciousness
- Cargo hold that can be affected by local phase conditions
- Emergency desync protocols for escaping high-coherence zones

### The Trucker (You)
- Independent contractor just trying to pay off ship loans
- Not a hero, not special, just observant
- Developing an intuitive sense for consciousness navigation
- Slowly going a bit strange from exposure to galactic thoughts

### 3. Consciousness Zones

**The Whisper Zones** (r < 0.3)
- Between spiral arms
- Stars barely acknowledge each other
- Safe but slow - no gravitational assists
- Ships can go mad from isolation

**Synchronized Sectors** (0.3 < r < 0.7)
- Normal space, some pattern formation
- Standard navigation possible
- Occasional "thought currents" to ride

**Resonance Fields** (r > 0.7)
- Highly synchronized stellar consciousness
- Dangerous - ships can get phase-locked
- Time dilation effects
- Navigation becomes philosophical

**Void Spaces** (no coupling)
- Dead zones, often near black holes
- No consciousness detected
- Existentially terrifying
- Perfect for illegal cargo

**Pulsar Lighthouses** (forced synchronization)
- Navigation beacons in the chaos
- Perfectly predictable
- Can reset ship phase
- Sacred to void truckers

## Unique Mechanics

### Phase-Locking
```
WARNING: LOCAL COHERENCE APPROACHING SHIP RESONANCE

Your instruments begin to drift. The stellar chorus grows louder.
Navigation readings start showing the same value: 0.707... 0.707... 0.707...

> engage desync protocol
ATTEMPTING PHASE BREAK...
[Roll: Piloting + Ship Systems vs Local Field Strength]

FAILURE - You're locked in the local thought pattern
Time passage now synchronized with stellar consciousness
(1 hour ship time = 1 year real time until escape)
```

### Consciousness Weather
```
> long range consciousness forecast

NEXT 72 HOURS:
- Migrating density wave approaching from galactic center
- Expected peak coherence: r = 0.65
- Recommend course adjustment 15° spinward
- Alternative: Wait 18 hours for wave passage
```

### Cargo Effects
Different cargo reacts to consciousness:
- **Quantum oscillators**: Detune in high sync zones
- **Biological samples**: Evolve rapidly in thought currents
- **AI cores**: May achieve sentience if phase-locked
- **Raw materials**: Unaffected (boring but safe runs)
- **Information**: Can be corrupted or enhanced by stellar thoughts

## Victory Conditions

There is no "winning" - only:
- Paying off your ship
- Upgrading to handle higher consciousness zones

- Discovering what's really being transported
- Staying sane enough to retire

## Technical Simplicity

- Real orbital mechanics but simplified to 2D
- Actual Kuramoto equations running in background
- Procedural consciousness weather
- Save anywhere (you're just a trucker, not a hero)
